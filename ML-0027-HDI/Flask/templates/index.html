Statistical Composite Index

Measuring a nation's progress by what it gives people, not what it produces.

The Human Development Index blends life expectancy, education, and income into a single score between 0 and 1. This tool trains a Linear Regression model on those three dimensions to estimate the HDI score and development tier for any set of country indicators.

The Three Dimensions of Development

01 — 03
01 — Health

Life Expectancy at Birth

A long, healthy life is treated as a precondition for everything else a person might do with their development.

02 — Knowledge

Mean & Expected Years of Schooling

Combines how much education adults have completed with how much a child entering school today can expect to receive.

03 — Living Standards

GNI Per Capita (PPP)

Gross National Income per person, adjusted for purchasing power, used as a proxy for command over economic resources.
